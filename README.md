# GramaAI – Voice-First Multilingual Generative AI Platform

## Overview

GramaAI is a voice-first, multilingual Generative AI assistant designed to make advanced AI accessible to rural and non-English users. The platform enables natural voice interaction in regional languages, eliminating typing and language barriers.

## Problem Statement

Most Generative AI platforms are English-centric and text-based. This limits access for users with low digital literacy, limited typing skills, and poor connectivity. As a result, rural communities, students, farmers, and small business owners are unable to benefit from AI-driven digital tools.

## Our Solution

GramaAI addresses this gap through:

- Voice-based interaction (Speech-to-Text & Text-to-Speech)
- Multilingual regional language support
- Domain-focused assistance (Agriculture, Education, MSMEs, Public Services)
- Low-bandwidth optimized design
- Scalable cloud deployment

The system transforms Generative AI into an inclusive digital assistant usable by anyone, regardless of language or technical skills.
